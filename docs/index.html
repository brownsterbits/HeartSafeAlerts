Yes. 100%. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no feature locks. Everything in Version 2.0 is free forever. We made a commitment: accessibility over profit.

üè• Is this a medical device?

No. HeartSafeAlerts is a wellness app, not a medical device. It's not FDA-cleared or clinically validated. Don't use it for medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergencies. Always consult healthcare professionals.

üì± What devices does it work with?

iPhone 8 or newer with iOS 18.4+. Works with any Bluetooth LE heart rate monitor (Polar, Wahoo, Garmin, Scosche, etc.) OR Apple Watch Series 4 or newer.

üîí What data do you collect?
