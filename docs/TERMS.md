## The Service

### What HeartSafeAlerts Does

HeartSafeAlerts is a heart rate monitoring application that:
- Connects to Bluetooth LE heart rate monitors
- Reads heart rate data from Apple Watch via HealthKit
- Displays real-time heart rate information
- Provides customizable alerts when heart rate is outside target range
- Tracks session statistics (min/max/avg, time in/out of range)

### What HeartSafeAlerts IS NOT

**HeartSafeAlerts is NOT:**
- A medical device
- A diagnostic tool
- A treatment or therapy
- A replacement for professional medical care
- FDA-cleared or CE-marked
- Intended for life-critical or emergency use

**Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.**
